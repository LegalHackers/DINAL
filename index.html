Do I Need a License? (DINAL?) is a web-based tool that offers quick, free guidance to creative people who want to use someone else's potentially copyrighted work in their own. Based on an algrithm that's currently in development, we'll return an opinion to you after you fill out the form below.

DINAL will initially be based on a very small sample: under 40 SCOTUS and 9th Circuit cases. With time, we hope to develop the data, algorithm and design to make the product more sophisticated.

NOTE THAT IT'S ALWAYS SAFEST TO ASK FOR CONSENT BEFORE KNOWINGLY USING SOMEONE ELSE'S WORK.
